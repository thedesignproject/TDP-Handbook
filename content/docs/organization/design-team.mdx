| Area | Description |

## Junior Designers

**Report to:** Luli or Delfi (exclusively — no skip-level communication)

**Communication:** Only through their assigned Lead Designer

<Callout type="warn">
Junior designers should never bypass their Team Lead for communication. All questions, issues, and updates flow through the assigned Lead Designer.
</Callout>
