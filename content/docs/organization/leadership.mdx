## Alex — TDP Director

- **Ultimate responsibility** for running TDP operations
- **Direct reports:** Dianne, Mica, Luli, Delfi
- **Focus:** Strategy, operational efficiency, people management support
- **Available for:** Management challenges, mentorship and escalations during the week (not just Mondays)

---

## Dianne — Commercial Lead

- **Primary:** All sales activities (new business, expansions, closing deals)
- **Sources:** YouTube leads, cold emails, referrals, expansion opportunities from Mica
- **Quality Control:** Weekly review of one random customer channel/deliverable
- **Design Input:** Participates in design reviews to provide feedback

---

## Mica — Customer Success Lead

- **Primary:** Customer success, recruiting, and CDO for customers

### Key Responsibilities

| Area | Description |
| :---- | :---- |
| Escalation | Valve for issues outside standard SOPs |
| Customer Check-ins | Monthly high-touch customer meetings |
| Expansion | Identifying opportunities (handoff to Dianne when ready) |
| CDO Plans | Managing CDO planning |
| Onboarding | Designers and customers |
| Recruitment | Hiring process |
| Quality Control | Weekly review of one random customer channel/deliverable |

---

## Luli & Delfi — Design Leads

- **Report to:** Alex
- **Manage:** Junior designers (exclusive communication channel)

### Key Responsibilities

| Area | Description |
| :---- | :---- |
| Design Operations | Day-to-day design work |
| Quality Management | Managing junior designer workload and quality |
| Customer Communication | Customer communication and deliverables |
| Customer Success | Escalate customer issues to Mica (support, not reporting line) |
| People Challenges | Escalate management challenges to Alex |

Can leverage Dianne and Mica as advisors for tough problems.
