## Overview

The Go-To-Market (GTM) Playbook defines TDP's strategic approach to launching products, services, and entering new markets. This playbook ensures alignment across commercial, operations, and design teams throughout the GTM process.

<Callout type="info">
This playbook is owned by Dianne (Commercial Lead) in collaboration with Mica (Customer Success & Ops) and Alex (Director).
</Callout>

---

## GTM Framework

### Purpose

- Define market positioning and value proposition
- Align internal teams on launch strategy
- Establish clear launch timelines and milestones
- Create repeatable processes for market entry

### Core Components

1. **Market Analysis** — Target audience, competitive landscape, positioning
2. **Value Proposition** — Customer problems solved, unique differentiation
3. **Launch Strategy** — Timing, channels, messaging, pricing
4. **Operational Readiness** — Team preparation, process documentation, tooling
5. **Success Metrics** — KPIs to measure GTM effectiveness

## GTM Process Stages

### 1. Strategy & Planning

**Owner:** Dianne (Commercial Lead)

| Activity | Description |
| :---- | :---- |
| Market Research | Identify target segments, size of opportunity, competitive analysis |
| Value Proposition | Define what we're offering and why it matters |
| Positioning | How we differentiate from alternatives |
| Pricing Strategy | Determine pricing model and tiers |

---

### 2. Internal Alignment

**Owner:** Cross-functional team (Dianne, Mica, Alex)

| Activity | Description |
| :---- | :---- |
| Team Briefing | Share GTM strategy with all team members |
| Process Documentation | Update SOPs to support new offering |
| Training | Prepare team for new capabilities or services |
| Resource Planning | Ensure capacity and skills are in place |

---

### 3. Launch Execution

**Owner:** Dianne (Commercial Lead) with support from Marketing/Operations

| Activity | Description |
| :---- | :---- |

| Customer Outreach | Initial contact with target customers |

---

### 4. Post-Launch Optimization

**Owner:** Mica (Customer Success & Ops) and Dianne (Commercial Lead)

| Activity | Description |
| :---- | :---- |

| Customer Feedback | Gather insights from early adopters |

| Team Retrospective | Share insights and document improvements |

## Success Metrics

Track the following metrics to measure GTM effectiveness:

- **Customer Acquisition:** New customers from GTM initiative
- **Revenue Impact:** Revenue generated within first 90 days
- **Market Penetration:** Percentage of target market reached
- **Customer Satisfaction:** NPS or satisfaction scores from new customers
- **Time to Value:** Days from sale to first customer success milestone
- **Team Efficiency:** Resource utilization and delivery quality
