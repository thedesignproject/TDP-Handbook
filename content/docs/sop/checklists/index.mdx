## When to Use These Checklists

### Lead - New Customer Assignment Checklist
Use when you are assigned as Lead to a new customer. Covers Day 0 (assignment) through Day 5-7 (Associate executing first task).

### Associate - New Customer Assignment Checklist
Use when you are assigned as Associate to a new customer. Covers Day 0 (assignment) through Day 5-7 (executing first task).
