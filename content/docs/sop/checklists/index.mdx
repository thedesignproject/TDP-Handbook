## When to Use These Checklists

### Customer Success Lead - New Customer Assignment Checklist
Use when a new customer deal is closing and needs designer assignment. Covers the week before customer joins through team assignment handoff.

### Lead - New Customer Assignment Checklist
Use when you are assigned as Lead to a new customer. Covers Day 0 (assignment) through first week (Associate executing first task).

### Associate - New Customer Assignment Checklist
Use when you are assigned as Associate to a new customer. Covers Day 0 (assignment) through first week (executing first task).
