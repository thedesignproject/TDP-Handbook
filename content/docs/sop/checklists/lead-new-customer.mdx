**Use this checklist when:** You are assigned as Lead to a new customer.

**Timeline:** Day 0 (assignment) through Day 5-7 (Associate executing first task)

**Related SOP:** [Step 1: Onboarding](/docs/sop/process/onboarding) | [Step 2: Partnership Setup](/docs/sop/process/partnership-setup) | [Step 3: Customer Kickoff](/docs/sop/process/customer-kickoff)

---

## Day 0: Assignment & Initial Review

<Callout type="info">
**Trigger:** Design Manager assigns you to a new customer after capacity assessment and customer-team assignment. See [Step 0: Before Kickoff](/docs/sop/process/before-kickoff).
</Callout>

### Your Actions

- [ ] **Receive assignment notification** from Design Manager
- [ ] **Review all customer documentation** from Sales/CS handoff:
  - Sales context and business goals
  - Customer Success expectation meeting notes (from Mica)
  - Customer stage assessment (compatibility matrix)
  - Operations transition plan (if applicable)
- [ ] **Verify workspace setup** created by Design Manager:
  - Asana project exists
  - Knowledge Tab populated with resources
  - Slack channel created and you're added
  - Figma project exists with correct file structure
- [ ] **Confirm Associate assignment** (if Lead+Associate model):
  - Check who is assigned as Associate
  - Verify Associate has been notified
  - Review Associate's current capacity

**Reference:** [Lead Responsibilities](/docs/sop/roles/lead) (60% Customer Relationship, 25% Strategy & UX, 15% Mentorship)

---

## Day 1: Associate Onboarding Setup

<Callout type="warn">
The Associate should receive workspace access (Asana, Figma, Slack) from the Design Manager BEFORE your partnership setup session. Confirm this is complete.
</Callout>

### Your Actions

- [ ] **Confirm Associate has workspace access:**
  - Asana project access
  - Figma project access
  - Customer Slack channel access
- [ ] **Schedule partnership setup session** (30 minutes) with Associate for Day 2-3
- [ ] **Prepare customer context summary** for Associate:
  - Customer business goals
  - Project scope and priorities
  - Key stakeholders
  - Customer communication preferences (from expectation meeting)
  - Current project stage
- [ ] **Review Lead delegation boundaries** for this customer:
  - What you'll own (strategy, customer relationship, quality approval)
  - What Associate will execute (design work, updates, dev handoff)
  - Refer to: [What Leads Own](/docs/sop/roles/lead#what-leads-own-cannot-delegate) and [What Can Be Delegated](/docs/sop/roles/lead#what-can-be-delegated-to-associates)

**Reference:** [Step 1: Onboarding](/docs/sop/process/onboarding) (Lead and Associate review all customer documentation)

---

## Day 2-3: Partnership Setup Session

<Callout type="info">
**Timeline:** 30-minute session with Associate. This happens BEFORE the customer kickoff meeting.
</Callout>

### Session Agenda (Based on Step 2: Partnership Setup)

- [ ] **Cal.com setup:** Set up the Cal.com link for the project so the customer can book meetings directly
- [ ] **Customer context review:**
  - Share customer business goals and priorities
  - Explain project scope and current stage
  - Introduce key stakeholders
- [ ] **Communication preferences:**
  - Daily EOD updates (Loom or Asana)
  - Weekly check-ins (if applicable)
  - How to ask questions (Slack for quick, Asana for task-related)
  - Response time expectations
- [ ] **Role boundaries for this customer:**
  - What Associate will own (execution, daily updates, iterations)
  - What Lead owns (strategy, customer meetings, final quality approval)
  - Decision-making framework: "Strategy vs. Execution"
- [ ] **Success metrics and growth opportunities:**
  - Quality standards for this customer
  - Growth Framework phase (Foundation: Months 1-3)
  - Path to increased autonomy
- [ ] **Establish feedback cadence:**
  - Daily feedback: After EOD updates or customer meetings
  - Bi-monthly feedback: Every two weeks (structured document)

**Reference:** [Step 2: Partnership Setup](/docs/sop/process/partnership-setup)

---

## Day 3-4: Kickoff Meeting Preparation

### Your Actions

- [ ] **Prepare kickoff meeting** using the **Kickoff Meeting Template** (Asana)
  - Customize structure to customer's specific needs
  - Review [kickoff framework](https://docs.google.com/document/d/10j2EZcGk_5KiZPBYkuqGh3Xsc3-tmwUaKIOhjSrvJ0A/edit?tab=t.0)
- [ ] **Decide Associate attendance** at kickoff:
  - Lead attendance: **Mandatory**
  - Associate attendance: **Optional** (decide based on project needs)
  - Refer to: [Meeting Attendance](/docs/sop/process/meeting-cadence#meeting-attendance)
- [ ] **Brief Associate** (if attending):
  - Meeting objectives
  - Associate role: Listen, learn customer voice, ask clarifying questions
  - Lead owns the meeting facilitation
- [ ] **Confirm meeting logistics:**
  - Calendar invite sent to customer
  - Fireflies enabled for recording
  - Asana notes template ready

**Reference:** [Step 3: Customer Kickoff](/docs/sop/process/customer-kickoff) (Lead prepares and owns kickoff meeting)

---

## Day 4-5: Customer Kickoff Meeting

<Callout type="info">
**Focus:** Understanding customer voice, priorities, and dynamics. Lead leads the meeting.
</Callout>

### During the Meeting

- [ ] **Lead the kickoff meeting:**
  - Follow prepared agenda
  - Establish rapport with customer
  - Understand customer priorities and goals
  - Clarify project scope and success criteria
  - Set communication expectations
- [ ] **Associate participates** (if attending):
  - Observes customer voice and dynamics
  - May ask clarifying questions when appropriate
- [ ] **Record meeting** (Fireflies or Granola)

### Post-Meeting

- [ ] **Document meeting notes** in Asana using Kickoff Meeting Template
- [ ] **Share recording** with Associate (if not in attendance)
- [ ] **Expect Associate's post-meeting questions:**
  - Associate watches recording within 24 hours
  - Associate submits summary and questions via Slack
  - Provide answers and context to Associate

**Reference:** [Step 3: Customer Kickoff](/docs/sop/process/customer-kickoff) (Associate submits summary and questions to Lead)

---

## Day 5: Meeting Cadence Setup

<Callout type="info">
The Head of Design discusses communication preferences with the customer during the expectation meeting. After kickoff, you finalize the schedule.
</Callout>

### Your Actions

- [ ] **Review customer's communication preference** (from expectation meeting):
  - Option 1: Weekly scheduled meeting (1x 45 min OR 2x 30 min/week)
  - Option 2: Office hours (dedicated weekly time block)
- [ ] **Send Slack message** in customer channel:
  - Confirm meeting setup approach
  - Share available time slots
- [ ] **Finalize schedule** with customer:
  - Add recurring meeting OR office hours block to calendar
  - Ensure visibility for accountability
- [ ] **Communicate meeting attendance expectations** to Associate:
  - First few months: Lead recommended, Associate required
  - After relationship established: Lead optional, Associate required

**Reference:** [Customer & Designer Meeting Cadence](/docs/sop/process/meeting-cadence) (Setup after kickoff meeting)

---

## Day 5-7: First Task Delegation

<Callout type="warn">
Target: Associate should be executing their first task within 3-5 business days of assignment.
</Callout>

### Your Actions

- [ ] **Define first task** for Associate:
  - Clear, achievable execution task (aligned with Foundation phase)
  - Provide context and direction
  - Set deadline and quality expectations
- [ ] **Assign task** in Asana with:
  - Clear description
  - Reference materials
  - Success criteria
  - Deadline
- [ ] **Clarify review process:**
  - How Associate submits work (Loom + Figma link)
  - When you'll provide feedback
  - Maximum revision expectations (2 revisions in Foundation phase)
- [ ] **Confirm Associate has everything needed:**
  - Access to all tools and resources
  - Understands the task
  - Knows how to ask questions
  - Ready to begin execution

**Reference:** [Associate Responsibilities](/docs/sop/roles/associate) (80% Design Execution, 10% Communication & Learning)

---

## Ongoing: First Week Monitoring

### Daily Expectations

- [ ] **Receive Associate's EOD update** (Loom or Asana)
- [ ] **Provide daily feedback:**
  - After reviewing EOD update
  - After customer meetings
  - Focus on execution quality and communication
- [ ] **Answer Associate questions** promptly:
  - Slack for quick questions
  - Asana for task-related questions
- [ ] **Monitor progress** toward first deliverable

### Week 1 Checkpoint

- [ ] **Review first deliverable** from Associate:
  - Provide clear, actionable feedback
  - Quality check against customer expectations
  - Confirm understanding of standards
- [ ] **Assess onboarding progress:**
  - Associate understands customer context
  - Associate following communication protocols
  - Associate meeting quality standards
  - Identify any gaps or support needs
