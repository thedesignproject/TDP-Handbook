**Use this checklist when:** A new customer deal is closing and needs designer assignment.

**Timeline:** Week before customer officially joins through assignment handoff

**Related SOP:** [Step 0: Before Kickoff](/docs/sop/process/before-kickoff) | [Sales to Operations Handoff](/docs/organization/processes#sales-to-operations-handoff) | [Meeting Cadence](/docs/sop/process/meeting-cadence)

---

## Sales Context & Early Visibility

<Callout type="info">
**Trigger:** Dianne (Sales) shares context after promising sales calls, before deal closure. This gives the team full visibility and context before payment confirmation.
</Callout>

### Your Actions

- [ ] **Receive sales context** from Dianne:
  - Sales call recordings
  - Customer needs and requirements
  - Project scope and complexity
  - Customer stage and business goals
  - Timeline expectations
- [ ] **Review customer information** in project management system:
  - Industry and product details
  - Stakeholders and decision-makers
  - Budget and engagement scope
  - Any special requirements or constraints
- [ ] **Assess customer fit** with TDP model:
  - Validate customer complexity fits model (no early-stage startups)
  - Confirm project aligns with team capabilities
  - Flag any concerns to Alex if needed

**Reference:** [Sales to Operations Handoff](/docs/organization/processes#sales-to-operations-handoff) (Dianne shares context immediately after promising sales calls)

---

## Capacity Assessment & Assignment Planning

<Callout type="info">
This happens before deal closure but after you have visibility into the potential customer. Plan for capacity needs.
</Callout>

### Your Actions

- [ ] **Check Lead capacity:**
  - Review Lead Capacity Dashboard (Asana Portfolio)
  - Identify Leads with capacity for new customer
  - Consider meeting load limits (~1 hour/week target per customer)
- [ ] **Check Associate availability:**
  - Review Associate capacity (3 customers standard, 4 as milestone)
  - Identify Associates with capacity
  - Consider current workload and performance
- [ ] **Plan Lead-Associate pairing:**
  - Assign an available Associate who is already paired with that Lead whenever possible
  - Each Associate should ideally have one consistent Lead
  - Avoid pairing Associates with multiple different Leads
- [ ] **Assess customer-team fit** using compatibility matrix:
  - Customer stage and complexity
  - Designer skills and experience level
  - Customer communication style
  - Project requirements

**Reference:** [Step 0: Before Kickoff](/docs/sop/process/before-kickoff) (Capacity Assessment & Customer-Team Assignment)

---

## Deal Closure & Expectation Meeting Preparation

<Callout type="warn">
Once deal is confirmed, move quickly to schedule and conduct the expectation meeting with the customer.
</Callout>

### Your Actions

- [ ] **Confirm deal closure:**
  - Payment confirmed
  - Contract signed
  - Customer ready to begin
- [ ] **Prepare for expectation meeting:**
  - Review all sales context and customer information
  - Prepare questions about customer preferences
  - Incorporate Lead Designer feedback (if assignment already determined)
  - Set up Granola or Fireflies for recording
- [ ] **Schedule expectation meeting** with customer:
  - Coordinate based on customer availability
  - Allow time before design team kickoff
  - Send calendar invite with agenda

**Reference:** [Sales to Operations Handoff](/docs/organization/processes#sales-to-operations-handoff) (Mica conducts expectation meeting with new customer)

---

## Expectation Meeting with Customer

<Callout type="info">
**Purpose:** Understand customer communication preferences, set expectations, and gather information for the design team handoff.
</Callout>

### Meeting Agenda

- [ ] **Discuss communication preferences:**
  - Ask customer preference: **Weekly scheduled meeting OR Office hours**
  - Understand customer's schedule predictability
  - Note any travel or availability constraints
- [ ] **Gather project context:**
  - Customer priorities and goals
  - Key stakeholders and decision-makers
  - Timeline expectations
  - Success criteria
  - Any specific requirements or constraints
- [ ] **Set expectations:**
  - Explain Lead + Associate model
  - Describe design process and workflow
  - Clarify communication channels (Slack, Loom, meetings)
  - Explain meeting cadence options
- [ ] **Record meeting:**
  - Use Granola or Fireflies
  - Take detailed notes
  - Document customer preferences and key information

**Reference:** [Customer & Designer Meeting Cadence](/docs/sop/process/meeting-cadence) (Step 1: Expectation Meeting - Ask customer preference: weekly meeting or office hours)

---

## Team Assignment Decision

<Callout type="info">
**Who Decides:** Luli/Delfi (Lead Designers) own assignment decisions based on project requirements, team skills/capacity, and customer-designer fit.
</Callout>

### Your Actions

- [ ] **Finalize Lead assignment:**
  - Based on capacity, skills, and customer fit
  - Consider customer stage and complexity
  - Ensure Lead has bandwidth for this customer
  - Document assignment decision
- [ ] **Finalize Associate assignment (if Lead+Associate model):**
  - Pair with Lead they already work with when possible
  - Confirm Associate has capacity (3 customers standard)
  - Consider Associate's skill level and growth phase
  - Document assignment decision
- [ ] **Create Operations Transition Plan** (simple written plan in Slack or short doc):
  - Which meetings Associate will join and starting when
  - How long the overlap will last
  - What part of the work Associate will take first
  - What the Lead continues to own during transition
- [ ] **Share transition plan** with Customer Success (Mica):
  - Communicate clearly with customer
  - Ensure alignment on expectations
  - Provide context for customer communication

**Reference:** [Step 0: Before Kickoff](/docs/sop/process/before-kickoff) (Operations Transition Plan) | [Sales to Operations Handoff](/docs/organization/processes#sales-to-operations-handoff) (Luli/Delfi own assignment decisions)

## Workspace Setup & Resource Preparation

<Callout type="warn">
Complete ALL workspace setup BEFORE notifying the Lead and Associate of their assignment. They should have everything ready to start immediately.
</Callout>

### Design Manager Setup Responsibilities

- [ ] **Create Asana project:**
  - Use standardized project template
  - Set up task structure
  - Add project to relevant portfolios
- [ ] **Set up Knowledge Tab in Asana:**
  - Add links to all relevant resources
  - Include sales context and recordings
  - Add customer documentation and branding
  - Link to any existing assets or research
- [ ] **Create Slack channel:**
  - Use naming convention: `#customer-name`
  - Add Lead Designer to channel
  - Add Associate (if assigned)
  - Prepare welcome message
- [ ] **Create Figma project:**
  - Set up correct file structure
  - Add Lead and Associate with appropriate permissions
  - Create initial files (branding, assets, etc.)
- [ ] **Grant workspace access to Associate:**
  - Asana project access
  - Figma project access
  - Slack channel access
  - Fireflies/meeting recordings access

**Reference:** [Step 0: Before Kickoff](/docs/sop/process/before-kickoff) (Design Manager Setup Responsibilities)

---

## Team Notification & Handoff

<Callout type="info">
Once workspace is fully set up, notify the assigned Lead and Associate. Provide complete context for smooth handoff.
</Callout>

### Your Actions

- [ ] **Notify Lead of assignment:**
  - Send assignment notification
  - Provide access to all customer resources
  - Share sales context, expectation meeting notes, and transition plan
  - Introduce Lead to customer assets, channels, and resources
  - Confirm Lead has everything needed to begin
- [ ] **Notify Associate of assignment (if applicable):**
  - Send assignment notification
  - Confirm workspace access is complete
  - Share customer context and resources
  - Communicate Lead-Associate pairing
- [ ] **Provide complete handoff documentation:**
  - Sales call recordings and context
  - Expectation meeting recording and notes
  - Customer communication preference (weekly meeting or office hours)
  - Operations transition plan
  - Customer stage and compatibility assessment
  - Any special requirements or constraints
- [ ] **Confirm handoff is complete:**
  - Lead has reviewed all documentation
  - Associate has access to all workspaces
  - Both are ready to begin onboarding process
  - Timeline is clear (3-5 days to first task execution)

**Reference:** [Step 0: Before Kickoff](/docs/sop/process/before-kickoff) (Introductions â€” Introduce Design Lead to all customer assets, channels, and resources)
