## Daily Cadence

### Associate: End-of-Day (EOD) Update

One update per customer, posted in the customer's internal channel on Slack:

- Work completed
- Design decisions made
- Questions or blockers
- Next day priorities

### Lead: Same-Day Feedback

- Same-day feedback on submissions when possible
- Otherwise morning-of feedback following the Associate's EOD update
- Schedules regular customer syncs (e.g., weekly meeting or office hours)
- Maintains alignment between customer expectations and internal work
- Organizes communication both with the customer and internally with the Associate
- Ensures feedback is shared promptly and tasks stay on-track

## Lead Weekly Requirements

### Progress review against customer timeline using the Unified Customer Progress Dashboard

- Review customer status (On Track / At Risk / Off Track)
- Check engagement score trends and weekly meeting load
- Review risk/blocker flags (timeline, capacity, scope creep)
- Identify where to push, adjust scope, or rebalance capacity
