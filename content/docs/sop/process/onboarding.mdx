---
title: "Step 1: Onboarding"
description: Initial onboarding process for Lead and Associate teams
---

**Owner:** Lead + Associate
**Timeline:** 1-2 days

## Onboarding Checklist

- Lead and Associate review all customer documentation
- Associate prepares context questions for the Lead
- Lead schedules expectation-setting session with Associate

<Callout type="info">
This step ensures both team members have full context before any customer interaction begins.
</Callout>
