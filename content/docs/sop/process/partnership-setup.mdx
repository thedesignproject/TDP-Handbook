---
title: "Step 2: Partnership Setup"
description: Establishing the working relationship between Lead and Associate
---

**Owner:** Lead
**Timeline:** 30 minutes

## Lead Establishes with Associate

- **Cal.com Setup:** Set up the Cal.com link for the project so the customer can book meetings directly without back-and-forth scheduling
- **Customer context** and business goals
- **Communication preferences** and schedules
- **Specific role boundaries** for this customer
- **Success metrics** and growth opportunities

## Clear Feedback Cadence

| Type | Frequency | Description |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Daily Feedback | During active work | After receiving Associate's EOD update or after customer meetings |
| Bi-monthly Feedback | Every two weeks | Structured feedback document shared between Lead and Associate, including retrospective on what's going well, what needs improvement, and areas for development |
