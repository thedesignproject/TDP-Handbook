**Owner:** Lead + Associate

## Initial Outreach

### Lead Introduction (Slack)

```
Hi team!
I'm [name], Design Lead at TDP, excited to start working with you all! :)
To kick things off, I'd love to schedule a 45 min meeting where I'll walk you though a Business Lean Canvas, to learn more about your business, goals, current pain points, and overall priorities and be set up for success for what's next!
Here are a few time options (All in [customer time zone]):
[option 1]
[option 2]
[option 3]
Let me know which time works best for you (if any), who you'd like to include, and I'll send over the invite.
Excited to get started!
```

### Associate Introduction (Slack)

```
I'm [Name], an Associate Designer at TDP, and I'll be your dedicated designer, mainly focused on execution.
Excited to start working with you all!
```

### First Call Introduction Template

```
Hi! I'm [Name], a [Role] at TDP, based in [Location].
My background is in [core discipline, e.g. Graphic Design / Product Design / UX], and I started my career [brief origin story — company, industry, or role]. I've also worked with [1–3 relevant companies, brands, or industries], where I focused on [key strength or type of work].
I joined TDP [X years/months] ago, and since then I've [career growth, promotion, or key responsibility]. One achievement I'm especially proud of is [specific professional win].
To back that up, [concrete metric or result — e.g. NPS, delivery speed, impact, scale].
In terms of how I work, I'm [1–2 soft skills: e.g. result-oriented, reliable, collaborative], and I care a lot about [customers, quality, clarity, ownership, etc.].
And to wrap up with a fun fact: [personal, light, human detail].
```

## Meeting Preparation

- Lead prepares the kickoff meeting in advance using the standardized **Kickoff Meeting Template** (Asana)
- Customize the structure to the specific needs of the customer

## During the Meeting

- Lead leads and owns the kickoff meeting
- Both Lead and Associate participate
- Associate may ask clarifying questions when needed
- **Focus:** Understanding customer voice, priorities, and dynamics

Associate submits summary and questions to Lead via the appropriate channel.
